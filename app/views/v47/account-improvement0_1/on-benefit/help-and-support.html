Help and support

If you need to manage your repayments, you can tell us how much you can afford to be taken from your Universal Credit payment.

If you're struggling to repay anything at all, you must contact Debt Management to discuss the options available to you.

Debt Management

  • Telephone: 0800 916 0647
  • Textphone: 0800 916 0651
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 7:30pm (except public holidays)

Further advice

  • help dealing with money problems from Citizens Advice (opens in new tab)
  • expert debt advice from StepChange Debt Charity (opens in new tab)
  • advice to improve your finances from Money Advice Service (opens in new tab)
